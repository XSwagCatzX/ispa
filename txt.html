Endagered species in Ontario

What is the problem

Endangered species are a big problem.
According to the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN) there are currentally 32 076 species that are either nearly threatened, vulneralble, endangered, critically endangered or extinct in the wild.
And there are already 866 species that are extinct. However, I don't want to focus on the entire world, I'm just going to focus on the endangered species in Ontario.

Currentally there are 215 species in Ontario that are either threatened, endangered or have special concerns.
